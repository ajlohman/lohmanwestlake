Dee Lohman, PH.D.

Dee Lohman has worked in public and private schools for over 20 years. She has a B.S. degree from Xavier University (Edgecliff College), M.S. and Ed.S degrees from Indiana University, and a Ph.D. degree from Purdue University. As a nationally certified school psychologist, Dee has a private practice license as well as public school licensure. She has served in various administrative roles in K-12 educational settings as well as teaching undergraduate and graduate courses at the university level.

Becky Westlake, M.S.

Becky Westlake has worked in public schools for over 20 years. She earned her B.S. degree from Ball State University and a M.S. degree from Indiana University. Becky has taught science at the middle and high school levels and served as a high school principal. She has also taught undergraduate university courses.
